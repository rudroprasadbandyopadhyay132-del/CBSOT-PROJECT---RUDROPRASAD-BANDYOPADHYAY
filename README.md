**📡 Telecom Customer Segmentation \& Risk Profiling**





Telecom Customer Segmentation is the strategic process of dividing a subscriber network into distinct cohorts based on usage behavior, account characteristics, and service friction. This project bridges the gap between predictive modeling and strategic outreach by engineering an end-to-end machine learning pipeline. Rather than stopping at a basic binary prediction, this architecture layers unsupervised machine learning over a class-balanced classifier to convert individual churn risk scores into robust, highly actionable customer segments.





By feeding variables such as account longevity, daytime billing charges, and customer service engagement thresholds into a K-Means Clustering engine, the project extracts distinct operational groups like "High-Risk Churn" and "Premium \& Loyal." The result provides data scientists, engineers, and product teams with a completely reproducible workflow to systematically isolate high-friction customer clusters, evaluate model stability, and deploy hyper-targeted retention strategies before revenue attrition occurs.
